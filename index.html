Watoto Kumi Children's Home

Home away from home

Watoto Kumi Children's Home aims to provide a home to the thousands of abandoned, homeless children of the ages between 0-15 years. It provides a family setting where the children get to develop in a loving and nurturing environment, lacking nothing that a normal child gets.

The home is situated in Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya.
